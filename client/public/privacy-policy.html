Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Scale Cases ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and handles information in connection with our internal advertising analytics platform.

Information We Collect

This platform is an internal tool used by authorized Scale Cases team members. It connects to the following services to retrieve and display advertising performance data:

  • Meta (Facebook) Ads API — campaign, ad set, and ad-level performance metrics including spend, impressions, clicks, and lead counts.
  • Google Sheets API — access to designated internal tracking spreadsheets for lead attribution and reporting.

We do not collect personal data from end users of this platform beyond what is necessary to authenticate and authorize access.

How We Use Information

Data retrieved through this platform is used solely for internal advertising performance analysis, budget pacing, and lead tracking. It is not shared with third parties or used for any purpose outside of Scale Cases internal operations.

Data Storage

Performance data is cached temporarily in server memory (up to 2 hours) to reduce API usage. No advertising performance data is stored in a persistent external database by this platform.

This platform interacts with the following third-party APIs under their respective terms of service and privacy policies:

  • Google Privacy Policy
